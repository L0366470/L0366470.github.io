Moonlight Suite

The Moonlight Suite is designed exclusively for couples seeking a quiet, intimate escape. Soft silver lighting, deep navy accents, and gentle moon‑inspired décor create a serene atmosphere perfect for romance and relaxation. This adult‑only suite offers complete privacy, luxurious comfort, and a peaceful ambiance that glows beautifully after sunset.

Enjoy a plush king‑size bed, cozy seating for two, and a private balcony where the moonlight reflects across the landscape. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply reconnecting, the Moonlight Suite provides the perfect setting for unforgettable moments together.
